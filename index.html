Everything you need to know about TMS therapy

TMS (Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation) is an FDA-cleared, non-invasive treatment that uses targeted magnetic pulses to stimulate underactive neurons in the prefrontal cortex — the brain region responsible for mood regulation. Each 19-minute session requires no anesthesia and has no systemic side effects.
